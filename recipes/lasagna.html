Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, ground meat (usually beef or pork), and a creamy mixture of ricotta and mozzarella cheeses. It’s often seasoned with herbs like basil and oregano, creating a hearty and comforting meal. Baked to perfection, the layers meld together into a savory, cheesy delight that’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions. Whether with meat or as a vegetarian version, lasagna is beloved for its deliciously satisfying flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Olive Oil
  • Onion chopped
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Red wine
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
