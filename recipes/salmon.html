Grilled Salmon Recipe

Grilled salmon is a flavorful and healthy dish, where the fish is cooked over an open flame or on a grill. The heat creates a crispy, slightly charred exterior while keeping the inside tender and juicy, with a rich pink color. Often seasoned with a simple mix of salt, pepper, and olive oil, grilled salmon can be enhanced with lemon, herbs, or a light glaze. It's a perfect balance of smoky, savory flavors, making it a popular choice for a satisfying, nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Lemons
  • Garlic
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Prepare the salmon
  2. Preheat the grill
  3. Grill the salmon
  4. Serve the salmon
